Envelope Contents

The ciphertext is a self-contained envelope. The EDK travels with the data — no separate key retrieval needed to decrypt.

EDK — Encrypted Data Key

DEK wrapped by KMS (CMK). Sent back to KMS on decrypt to recover the DEK.

-

IV — Nonce

Random 12-byte AES-GCM nonce. Unique per encryption, never reused.

-

CT — Ciphertext

AES-256-GCM ciphertext of the actual field value. Useless without the DEK.
